Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a historical monument located in Agra, India. It is constructed on the bank of the Yamuna River and includes an area of about 17 hectares. It is one of the seven wonders of the world. Shah Jahan, a Mughal emperor, built the Taj Mahal in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

The construction started in 1199 by Qutub-ud-din Aibak and was completed by Ittutimish in 1220. It is considered as the symbol of victory as it earmarked the beginning of the Delhi Sultanate in India. Aibak was unable to complete the minaret.

Red Fort

The Red Fort in Delhi, India was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan. He commissioned the construction in 1638 after moving his capital from Agra to Delhi
